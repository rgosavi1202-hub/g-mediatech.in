Political Profile

Jevan Chakankar

Indian National Congress
Vice President – OBC Department, Pune City

In the backdrop of the Pune Municipal Corporation General Elections 2026, Jevan Chakankar is the official candidate of the Indian National Congress, a constituent party of the Maha Vikas Aghadi.

Since joining the Congress party in 2008, he has remained deeply involved in public life, working at the grassroots level for social justice, inclusive development, and people-centric governance.

His dedication, integrity, and continuous engagement with citizens have earned him trust and respect across Pune city.

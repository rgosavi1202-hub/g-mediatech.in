Political Profile

In the backdrop of Pune Municipal Corporation General Elections 2026, Jevan Chakankar is the official candidate of the Indian National Congress. He has been actively involved in public service since 2008.

Currently serving as Vice President, OBC Department, Pune City Congress Committee, he works for social justice, inclusive development, and citizen welfare.
